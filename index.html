Important Safety Information

This website provides educational information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

If you are in crisis or think you may have an emergency, call your doctor or 911 immediately.

Always seek the advice of your mental health professional or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding your condition.

Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

If you are considering harming yourself or others, please contact emergency services immediately.

About Adzentura

Learn about our mission, founder, and commitment to mental health education

DC

Dylan Castro

Founder & Developer

I believe everyone deserves free access to accurate, evidence-based information about mental health disorders. As someone who has seen the impact of mental health challenges in my own community, I created Adzentura to provide a comprehensive resource hub for students, educators, and anyone seeking to better understand mental health.

Adzentura is a non-profit initiative dedicated to breaking down barriers to mental health education. Our mission is to provide accessible, reliable information that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their mental wellbeing.

This website is independently owned and developed by me, with the goal of creating a safe, informative space that bridges the gap between clinical knowledge and everyday understanding of mental health.

Our Mission

Adzentura is committed to providing free, evidence-based mental health resources to students, educators, and the general public. We believe that education is a powerful tool in reducing stigma and improving mental health outcomes.

All content on this site is carefully researched and presented in an accessible format. While we strive for accuracy, we always emphasize that our resources are educational and not a substitute for professional medical advice.

As a non-profit initiative, our focus is solely on providing value to our users. We do not accept advertising or sponsored content that could compromise the integrity of our information.
