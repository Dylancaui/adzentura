Dylan Castro

Founder & CEO, Adzentura

Dylan Castro founded Adzentura in 2025 with a singular focus: to build a world-class holding company from the ground up. While not yet recognized in public markets or institutional rankings, Dylan brings deep strategic thinking, operational discipline, and a relentless commitment to long-term value creation. Prior to Adzentura, Dylan worked on independent ventures and studied business architecture & systems design.

As a first-time founder in the holding space, Dylan is currently shaping Adzentura's core blueprint — from legal structure to portfolio strategy. The company operates with zero debt and a lean philosophy, targeting a full ecosystem launch in 2027. Dylan's leadership is rooted in transparency, patience, and the belief that enduring enterprises are built one brick at a time.

“We are not recognized today, but recognition is never the goal — building something that outlasts us is.” — Dylan Castro
